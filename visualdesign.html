Art Piece #2 - Canva Art

I used the program Adobe Express to make a collage of this I feel is my ¨aesthetic¨. I started by finding pictures online and downloading them which was a bit time consuming because the files kept duplicating and some of the files were not compatible with Adobe Express, so I had to make the collage as I searched for pictures. I included the pictures that I did because purple and blue have always been my favorite colors and I find them soothing. I think my collage matches my aesthetic because I´ve been a fan of elements of the earth like the ocean whilst also liking technology and lights.
